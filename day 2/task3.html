div

A div is a block-level element. This means it starts on a new line. It is typically used for grouping larger sections of content and applying styles to entire blocks of elements.

span

A span is an inline element. This means it does not start on a new line and only occupies as much width as its content requires. It is primarily used for styling without affecting the overall layout.
